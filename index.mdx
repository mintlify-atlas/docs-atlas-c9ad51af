ZenTao is a comprehensive, open-source project management platform built for software development teams. It unifies product planning, sprint execution, bug tracking, quality assurance, and DevOps tooling in a single self-hosted application — giving your team full ownership of data and workflows.

## Get started in 3 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install ZenTao">
    Download and run ZenTao on your server, or pull the Docker image. See the [installation guide](/installation) for all deployment options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your product and project">
    Add a product to define your backlog of stories, then create a project to plan and execute work. Follow the [quickstart guide](/quickstart) for a step-by-step walkthrough.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your team and start a sprint">
    Add team members, assign roles, and kick off your first sprint or execution. ZenTao coordinates developers, testers, and product owners in one place.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  ZenTao is available in three editions: **Open Source** (free, AGPL/ZPL licensed), **Biz** (commercial, adds OA and operations management), and **Max** (enterprise, adds CMMI, risk management, and advanced metrics). This documentation covers the Open Source edition.
</Note>
